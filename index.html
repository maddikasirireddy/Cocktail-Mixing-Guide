  • Margarita

    • Ingredients:

    • 45 ml tequila
    • 30 ml triple sec
    • 30 ml lime juice
    • Salt for rim
    • Ice cubes

    Steps:

    • Rub lime around the rim of the glass and dip in salt.
    • Shake tequila, triple sec, and lime juice with ice.
    • Strain into a chilled glass.
    • Garnish with a lime wheel.
